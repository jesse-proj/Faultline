Poverty Distribution Heatmap

An interactive geographic visualization tracking the percentage of populations living below the national poverty line. Use your mouse to pan, zoom, and explore specific regions.

Global Extreme Poverty Index

Data reflects estimated percentages of populations severely deprived of basic resources (World Bank / UN projections).

Source: Consolidated Global Economic Reports (2024-2025) Scale: % of Total Population

Analyzing the Map

The visual weight of the Global South is immediately apparent in this projection. Nations carrying the deepest shades of red correspond heavily with regions subjected to historical colonization and ongoing unequal exchange in global trade architectures.

Data Limitations

Poverty is multidimensional. This heatmap primarily tracks fiscal poverty lines and may not fully represent situational poverty, lack of internet access, or localized purchasing power disparities in otherwise "wealthy" nations.
